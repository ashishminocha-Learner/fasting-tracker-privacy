Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 6, 2026

1. No Data Collection

Zazen· Fasting Tracker does not collect, transmit, or share any personal data. The app has no backend server, no analytics, and no advertising.

2. Data Stored on Your Device

All data is stored locally on your device only:

  • Fasting session history (start time, end time, duration)
  • App preferences and notification settings

This data never leaves your device.

3. Notifications

The app may request permission to send local notifications for fasting milestones, hydration reminders, and daily reminders. These are generated on-device only and no data is sent to any server.

4. Live Activities

The app uses Apple's Live Activities to show your fasting timer on the Lock Screen. All processing happens on-device and is not shared with us or any third party.

5. Third-Party Services

The app does not use any third-party analytics, advertising, or data collection services.

6. Children's Privacy

The app does not collect any information from anyone, including children under 13.
